Huntington County Help Board

Midge Decker

Midge is a retired pharmacist. She is a member of the Optimist Club, Altrusa International, Pathfinder Services, Huntington County Free Health Clinic Board member, Huntington County Community Foundation Board member, YMCA Foundation Board member and the REMC Operation Roundup. Midge is a member of Trinity United Methodist Church. She is the mother of two sons and has four grandchildren.

David Dyer

Dave began his chiropractic health care business in Huntington in 1979. He is a 34 year member of the Huntington Rotary club and has served as president. Dave is an active member of the Indiana State Chiropractic Association and served as its President in 1992. Dave was named Chiropractor of the Year by the Indiana State Chiropractic Association in 1993. In addition, he has served as president of the Indiana State Chiropractic Licensing Board for the past 15 years. Dave and his wife, Debbie, are the parents of two sons and have two granddaughters. They attend Emmanuel Community Church.

Judy Frischman

Judy is a retired teacher and has taught children with special needs through Pathfinder Services and Huntington County Community Schools. Judy serves as treasurer of the Board of Directors for Youth Services Bureau and is a member of the Human Rights Committee for Pathfinder Services. Judy is a member of Tri Kappa Associates, First Mother's Club and attends Christ the King Episcopal Church. Judy also serves as a volunteer at the Honeywell Center. Judy and her husband, Ron have eight children and several grandchildren.
